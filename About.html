Who Is Vivlia?

We are a start-up that aims to provide college students with a more social, low-cost, and stress-free system to get their book. All Vivlia members are either recent graduate or current students. At Vivlia, we know the stress and frustration that comes every semester regarding textbooks. With high markup prices from publishers and bookstores, Vivilia creates a low cost alternative for college students to get their textbooks. By buying, selling, or trading previously used textbooks from courses the individual already taken to their peers, both parties will benefit. Buyers will be able to make money or acquiring a require textbook for the approaching semester from their used books and sellers can get the books they need at a reasonable price or exchange their used books with other peers.



How It Work?


1. Create an account

2. Enter your General Info (School, Major, and Contact info)

3. Enter your Current Semester courses (Class, Class name, Class #, section, Professor) to find require books for those courses.

4. Choose the book that you want under each Course

5. Contact the Lister for more info on pricing and terms (can buy or trade books with the lister)

6. Establish a meeting place within school using contact info.

7. Exchange your books.

8. Repeat 3-7 each new semester
