Welcome

Vivlia is a platform for college students attending the same school to buy, sell, & trade textbooks and create a sharing community within their college. At Vivlia, we aim to help students save money on textbooks, reduce the stress of finding required textbooks at a low price, and meet new peers through Vivlia platform.

Find TextBooks
with Ease

THE MAIN GOAL FOR VIVLIA IS TO PROVIDE AN ALTERNATIVE WAY FOR STUDENTS TO BUY BOOKS AT A LOWER COST THAN THE COLLEGE BOOKSTORE AND BOOK RETAILERS.

Looking To Sell
Your Books?

All you have to do is post the textbook for sale or trade with Title, Author, Edition, IBSN #, Course (e.g. MATH101), and Course Name (e.g. Pre-Calculus). Student looking to buy will contact you through email.
